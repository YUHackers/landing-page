We love hacking building breaking things YUHackers

YU Hackers is a club dedicated to help students build interesting things with hardware and software

Our goal is to embody an environment in YU with the hacker culture of move fast and break things, and learn by doing.




Events

Our events happen twice a week (On Sunday and Thursday nights) in the style of hacker hours: Everyone is invited, you come with your laptop, hangout and code.

Flag down one of the mentors if you have a question. That's it! If we can't answer your question we'll find you someone who can, or at least point you in the right direction. We also have arduinos and hardware everyone can use.

Find us hacking on the 5th floor in the Rubin Study Lounge
